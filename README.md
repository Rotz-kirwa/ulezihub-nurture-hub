# Jifunze Ulezi Hub - Nurture Hub

## About the Project

Jifunze Ulezi Hub is a comprehensive maternal health support platform dedicated to serving pregnant and lactating women in Kenya. The platform provides expert guidance, personalized care, and compassionate support throughout the motherhood journey.

**Website**: [www.jifunzeulezi.co.ke](https://www.jifunzeulezi.co.ke)

## Mission

Nurturing Healthy Motherhood, Fatherhood and Childhood Experience

We bridge the gap between routine clinical care and the holistic support mothers truly need during pregnancy, childbirth, and lactation. Our expert team reaches women in virtual spaces and in-person to offer customized, personalized compassionate maternal support.

## Services Offered

### Core Services
1. **Preconception Counseling** - Preparing body and mind for a healthy pregnancy journey
2. **Pregnancy Journey & What to Expect** - Month-by-month guidance through pregnancy
3. **Education at Childbirth & What to Expect** - Understanding labor, delivery, and postpartum care
4. **Infant Feeding Support** - Breastfeeding support and infant nutrition guidance (1st 2 weeks & up to 6 months)
5. **HIV Context in Pregnancy and Lactation** - Specialized support for HIV-positive mothers
6. **Maternal Mental & Emotional Support** - Addressing anxiety, depression, and emotional wellness
7. **Men & Boy Role in Maternal Health** - Engaging fathers and boys in supporting maternal health
8. **Community Individualized Expert Support** - Personalized support from our team of experts

## Technology Stack

This platform is built with modern web technologies to ensure a fast, responsive, and accessible experience:

- **Frontend Framework**: React with TypeScript
- **Build Tool**: Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with custom maternal health theme
- **UI Components**: shadcn-ui
- **Routing**: React Router with future flags
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display (serif) and Inter (sans-serif)
